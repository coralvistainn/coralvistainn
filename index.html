About Us

A Riverside Haven in the 'Ooty of Kerala'

Coral Vista Inn is situated in the breathtaking landscape of Kariyathumpara, often celebrated as the "Ooty of Kerala" for its pristine beauty. Nestled near the foothills of Kakkayam, our resort offers a panoramic view of lush greenery and crystal-clear waters.

Whether you are looking for a family picnic spot, a romantic getaway, or a base to explore the famous Thonikkadavu, we provide the perfect blend of traditional Kerala hospitality and modern comfort.

  • Located right next to the river
  • Eco-friendly architecture
  • Access to Kakkayam Dam trekking
